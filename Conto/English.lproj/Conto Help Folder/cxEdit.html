Edit records

Double click on an entry field to modify it. You can toggle the checkmark of an entry by clicking over the corresponding cell. To remove records, simply select the record(s) you want to delete and choose Delete from the Edit menu or hit the Delete key. Use the Shift key to select multiple records and use the Command key to select non-adjacent records.

Income and Expense fields are not mutually exclusive: this means that you can freely put both an income and an expense in the same record. Simply double-click on a field and fill it in.

You can rearrange and resize the columns as you like. Conto will remember your settings.

You can sort entries based on the values of a given column by clicking on the corresponding column header. You can also move records by dragging and dropping them. You can copy records from one document to another by using drag and drop. You can cut, copy and paste records among documents by selecting the corresponding menu items in the Edit menu. Choose Copy as Text in the Edit menu to copy the selected records as tab-separated plain text.
