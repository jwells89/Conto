Conto Help

Thank you for trying Conto!

Conto is a simple application that allows you to keep track of your expenses and incomes on a monthly basis. Its features include

  • support for different number formats, date formats and currencies
  • strong encryption (requires GnuPG)
  • support for storing any account-related information
  • full undo-redo capabilities
  • drawing histogram from your data
...and more.

Choose a topic on the left menu to know more.
