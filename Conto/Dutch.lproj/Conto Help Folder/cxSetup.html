Setup the Application

No setup is necessary, but you may want to edit the preferences to comply with your requirements. By default, Conto uses the euro currency symbol after the number, the comma as a decimal separator, the period as a thousand separator and two decimal digits. You may quickly and flexibly modify all these settings in the Preferences window.

In the Preferences you can also change the date format, choose to show/hide a grid in the tables, to use a smaller or bigger font, to use encryption to protect your documents and to add or remove predefined descriptions.
